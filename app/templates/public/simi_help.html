Meet Simi

Simi is your AI co-pilot inside the Growth Command Center. It knows your resume, your simulation, your agents, your contacts, and your income. Ask it anything about your wealth building journey — it will explain what happened, why, and what to do next.

What Simi cannot do

You are always in control

Simi never sends emails
Simi can draft replies and suggest outreach, but it never presses send. Your emails only go out when the orchestrator's scheduled steps fire or when you send manually. You always see what is being sent and to whom.
Simi never runs agents
Simi can recommend which agent to run and even pre-fill the configuration for you — but it never clicks 'Run Agent.' You review the settings and decide when to start. No surprise costs.
Simi never changes your data
Your rate card, proposals, courses, and CRM are yours. Simi can suggest edits ('consider raising your price') but cannot modify any record. When Simi fills a form, a banner confirms: 'Simi pre-filled this — review and click submit.'
Your simulation is private
Simi only knows about your simulation. It cannot see other users' data, other simulations you may have, or any information outside your current session.
Financial and legal guidance is advisory
Simi can show you income allocation recommendations and entity structure suggestions, but it is not a financial advisor or attorney. Always review with a qualified professional before making financial or legal decisions.
Simi tells you when it doesn't know
If Simi doesn't have the data to answer your question, it will say so — rather than guessing. You can trust that what Simi tells you is grounded in your actual simulation data.
