FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

Your free bio page is AI-generated from your resume and includes a professional bio, a 24/7 AI chat widget that answers questions from visitors on your behalf, a lead capture form that automatically saves contacts to your CRM, SEO meta tags, and three design themes. No credit card required — free forever.
When a visitor clicks "Chat with [your name]" on your bio page, they connect to an AI assistant trained on your resume, services, and expertise. It answers questions about your background and availability, and automatically captures their name and email into your CRM — even at 2am when you're not available.
A simulation is a complete AI-powered analysis of your career. Our Bayesian orchestrator identifies your Expertise Zone and deploys 49 specialized agents across 6 income layers — from active consulting (Layer 1) to passive wealth deployment (Layer 5). Each agent generates a specific deliverable: rate card, cold email campaign, course outline, blog post, and more.
It is a one-time payment per simulation — not a subscription. You pay {{ sim_price_usd }} once, receive all 49 deliverables, and keep access to your results indefinitely. Want to run a second simulation on a different expertise zone or updated resume? That is another {{ sim_price_usd }}, separately.
Your resume and career data are processed solely to generate your bio page and simulation. We never sell your data to third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your Settings page. Your bio page visitors' contact information is stored in your private CRM and is never shared.
No — all integrations are optional. Simulacrum works fully without connecting any third-party tool. Connecting Cal.com, Kajabi, Apollo, or Stripe simply unlocks richer automation for each deliverable. Your bio page and AI chat are live from the moment you upload your resume.
