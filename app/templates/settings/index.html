Compliance

CAN-SPAM (US) — Every email Simulacrum deploys includes your business address (from Settings → Profile → Location) and an Apollo-managed one-click unsubscribe link. Sequences cannot be deployed until your business address is set. Hard bounces and unsubscribes permanently block re-outreach to that contact.

GDPR (EU contacts) — If your target contacts are located in the European Union, consult with a legal advisor before enabling outreach. Simulacrum's lawful basis is legitimate interest for B2B outreach, but attorney review is required before sending to EU-located contacts at scale.
